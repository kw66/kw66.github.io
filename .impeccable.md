## Design Context

### Users
Academic collaborators, reviewers, recruiters, and technically serious visitors who need to understand Xulin Li's research direction and representative work quickly.

### Brand Personality
Credible, thoughtful, evidence-oriented. The site should feel like a research homepage with taste, not a startup landing page or a flashy portfolio.

### Aesthetic Direction
Warm editorial academic style. Light mode by default. Serif display typography paired with a clean sans-serif body font, restrained motion, and a structured but not rigid layout.

### Design Principles
1. Lead with clarity over novelty.
2. Emphasize research direction and representative projects, not decorative metrics.
3. Keep the hierarchy obvious on both desktop and mobile.
4. Use visual polish to support credibility, not to overpower content.
5. Make the root user site and existing project site feel related but clearly distinct.

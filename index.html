Research

Research interests that stay close to real use.

I am most interested in visual retrieval problems where the setting is less tidy than a benchmark table suggests. That includes person re-identification, retrieval under changing conditions, and practical pipelines for turning experiments into something understandable and reusable.

Beyond model performance, I care about how work is presented: figures, posters, diagrams, and project pages are not decoration to me. They are part of the research interface.
